Reflections & Future Guidance

University Journey Reflection

My time at University of Macau has been transformative, combining rigorous academic study with valuable extracurricular experiences. The CKLC residential college activities provided holistic development opportunities beyond the classroom, teaching me the importance of community engagement and work-life balance.

Key Insight: Technical skills alone are insufficient for success; communication, collaboration, and emotional intelligence are equally crucial in professional environments.

Internship Experience Reflection

The internship at Municipal Affairs Bureau (I.A.M.) provided practical insights into government operations and data management. It reinforced the importance of accuracy, attention to detail, and systematic approaches to problem-solving.

Key Learning: Even seemingly routine administrative tasks require precision and can benefit from technical optimization through tools like Excel macros and systematic verification processes.

Technical Development Reflection

My progression in computer science has shifted from learning programming basics to applying technical knowledge to real-world problems. The combination of coursework, self-study (through platforms like Bilibili), and practical projects has created a comprehensive learning ecosystem.

Key Realization: Continuous learning is essential in technology fields, and proactive skill development (like learning Python beyond required coursework) creates competitive advantages.
