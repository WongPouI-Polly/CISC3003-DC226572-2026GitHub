Our History

How LW Creative Studio came to be

LW Creative Studio was founded in early 2026 when two University of Macau computer science students — Leong Chi Long and Wong Pou I — discovered a shared passion for web design during their CISC3003 Web Programming course.

What started as a class project quickly evolved into a professional partnership. Recognising the growing demand for quality web design services in Macau's digital economy, they decided to combine their complementary skills and launch a freelance studio.

Today, LW Creative Studio specialises in responsive, accessible websites for small businesses and startups, with a focus on clean design, modern code, and exceptional client service.
